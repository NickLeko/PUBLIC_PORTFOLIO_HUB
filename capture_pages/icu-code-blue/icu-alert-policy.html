Why this artifact matters

Alert budgets stay visible

The reference run reports 10,391 raw alerts at the naive top 0.5% threshold. Policy shaping cuts that to 2,650 with the debounced policy.

Precision is still modest

Even the best checked policy stays in retrospective, low-prevalence territory at 0.264%. That boundary belongs in the artifact, not hidden in footnotes.

Scope boundary

This remains a held-out-hospital ranking study using a chart-derived proxy label. It is useful for reasoning about tradeoffs, not for claiming clinical deployment readiness.
